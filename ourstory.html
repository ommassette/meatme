Established Excellence

The Pursuit of Gastronomic Perfection

Born out of an uncompromising obsession with fine dining, Meat Me in Kilimani emerged to challenge standard meat provisions in Nairobi. We recognized a deep artistic gap between mass industrial supply lines and the absolute demands of elite culinary craft.

Every cut we secure tells an intentional story of immaculate pasture management, rigorous cattle bloodlines, and customized biological maturation cycles spanning up to 45 separate days within customized dry-aging vaults.

We operate not merely as merchants, but as culinary curators. Our Master Butchers systematically dissect muscle structures with mathematical refinement, guaranteeing a melt-in-the-mouth texture profile across every single customized order block.
